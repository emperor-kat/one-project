# Refrigerator Types: Complete Comparison

## Main Categories

!!! success "Expert Choice"
    For Barnaul's climate, we recommend No-Frost models for their reliability.

### 1. Single Door

**Best for:**

- Small families (1-2 people).

- Limited kitchen space.

- Budget buyers.

**Features:**

- Capacity: 150-250L.

- Price: 15,000-25,000₽.

- Energy: A+ to A++.

### 2. Two Door (Top Freezer)

**Advantages:**

- Better organization.

- More affordable than bottom freezer.

- Energy efficient.

### 3. Side-by-Side

- **Capacity**: 350-600L.

- **Features**: Water dispenser, ice maker.

- **Price**: 40,000-100,000₽.

- **Best for**: Large families.


## Technology Comparison

=== "Direct Cool vs No-Frost"
    | Aspect | Direct Cool | No-Frost |
    |--------|-------------|----------|
    | Defrosting | Manual required | Automatic |
    | Maintenance | High | Low |
    | Price | Lower | Higher |
    | Efficiency | Good | Excellent |
