# Refrigerator Types: Complete Comparison

## Main Categories

!!! success "Expert Choice"
    For Barnaul's climate, we recommend No-Frost models for their reliability.

### 1. Single Door

![Single Door](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1595428270785-585cbd49086f?w=300){ align=left width=200 style="margin-right: 20px;" }

**Best for:**
- Small families (1-2 people)
- Limited kitchen space
- Budget buyers

**Features:**
- Capacity: 150-250L
- Price: 15,000-25,000₽
- Energy: A+ to A++

### 2. Two Door (Top Freezer)

**Advantages:**
- Better organization
- More affordable than bottom freezer
- Energy efficient

### 3. Side-by-Side

<div class="cards" markdown>

- **Capacity**: 350-600L
- **Features**: Water dispenser, ice maker
- **Price**: 40,000-100,000₽
- **Best for**: Large families

</div>
