# Cats
## Treat Recipes

=== "Tuna Bites"
    ```yaml recipe
    ingredients:
      - 1 can tuna
      - 1 tbsp catnip
    steps:
      - Mix ingredients
      - Shape into bites
      - Serve immediately
    ```

=== "Chicken Delights"
    ```yaml recipe
    ingredients:
      - 1/2 cup cooked chicken
      - 1 tsp salmon oil
    steps:
      - Shred chicken
      - Drizzle with oil
      - Serve at room temperature
    ```
