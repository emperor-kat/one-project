#  Refrigerators

Refrigerators are essential kitchen appliances for preserving food. They work by removing heat from the interior and releasing it to the surrounding environment.

## Types of refrigerators

There are several  types  of refrigerators:

* Top freezer
* Bottom freezer
* French door
* Side-by-Side
* smart models that connect to Wi-Fi.

You can learn more by searching online [here](https://www.example.com).

> Important: Always ensure the door is closed properly.

| Feature | Benefit |
| --- | --- |
| Energy Star Rating | Saves money on electricity bills |
| Adjustable Shelves | Customizes storage space |
| Built-in Water Dispenser | Provides cool water and ice |
